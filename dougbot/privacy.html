DougBot - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [03/05/2025]

1. What We Collect

DougBot may temporarily receive:

  • Message content (when you type commands like !doug)
  • User ID and Server ID (through Discord's API)

We do not store or log this info.

2. What We Don't Collect

  • No passwords
  • No private messages
  • No IP addresses
  • No personal or sensitive data

3. How We Use Data

Only to respond with chaos (e.g., memes, emojis, quotes). Nothing else.

4. Third Parties

DougBot runs via Discord’s API, so you’re also bound by Discord's Privacy Policy.

5. Contact

Questions, concerns, or fan mail? DM Marti Boa on Discord.
