Tyler Londragan

A Proud Fan of Aberdeen and its Football Team

About Tyler

Tyler Londragan is a devoted supporter of Aberdeen Football Club, a team that has been at the heart of Scottish football for generations. Born and raised in Aberdeen, Tyler has always felt a deep connection to the city and its iconic football culture.

Why Aberdeen?

Aberdeen isn’t just a city; it’s a community rich in history, strength, and resilience. For Tyler, supporting Aberdeen FC is more than cheering on a team—it’s about celebrating the spirit of his hometown and its people.

Aberdeen Football Club

Aberdeen FC, known for its fiery passion and loyal fanbase, has won the hearts of many, including Tyler. From legendary players to unforgettable matches, the team embodies the pride and spirit of the city. Tyler attends every home game he can, proudly donning the red and white.
