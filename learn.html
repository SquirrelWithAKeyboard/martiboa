Learn More About German Film

Expressionism: A Visual Revolution

German Expressionism brought a unique visual style to cinema, characterized by distorted sets, sharp contrasts, and intense themes. This movement greatly influenced Hollywood film noir and horror genres.

Key Figures in German Cinema

Renowned directors like Fritz Lang ("M") and Wim Wenders ("Wings of Desire") made an indelible mark on both German and global film history.

Berlinale: Germany's Film Celebration

One of the world's most prominent film festivals, the Berlinale, showcases diverse films and sets trends for the international film industry.
